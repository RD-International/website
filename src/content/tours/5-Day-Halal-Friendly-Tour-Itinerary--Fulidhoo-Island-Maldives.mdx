Day 1: Arrival and Island Welcome

- Speedboat transfer from Malé to Fulidhoo Island.
- Check-in at a halal-friendly guesthouse with modest facilities and privacy.
- Leisurely island walk: explore the quiet village streets, meet the friendly locals, and visit the mosque.
- Sunset beach walk and relaxation.

Day 2: Snorkeling and Marine Discovery

- Morning guided snorkeling excursion to explore vibrant coral reefs and tropical marine life in a safe, family-friendly environment.
- Afternoon beach time with access to secluded areas or private spaces for modest swimwear.
- Evening relaxation with non-alcoholic refreshments and a halal local dinner.

Day 3: Island Hopping and Cultural Exploration

- Full-day island hopping tour to nearby inhabited and uninhabited islands.
- Enjoy a picnic lunch with halal-certified meals in our guesthouse.
- Learn about traditional island life, craftsmanship, and the Maldivian fishing heritage.
- Return to Fulidhoo for a peaceful evening.

Day 4: Sandbank Escape and Secluded Leisure

- Morning: Depart for a private sandbank excursion — a pristine stretch of white sand surrounded by turquoise waters, perfect for quiet reflection and nature appreciation. Enjoy swimming or relaxing in a secluded environment, ideal for modest swimwear and privacy.
- Midday: Picnic lunch prepared with halal-certified ingredients, served on the sandbank with shaded seating.
- Afternoon: Free time for personal leisure — whether it’s swimming, reading, photography, or simply enjoying the peaceful ambiance of the open sea.
- Return to Fulidhoo by late afternoon for rest and a light dinner at the guesthouse.

Day 5: Farewell and Departure

- Breakfast at the guesthouse.
- Free time for final souvenir shopping or photos.
- Transfer by speedboat back to Malé.
